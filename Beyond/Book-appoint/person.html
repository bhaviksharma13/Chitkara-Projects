Our Commitment:

Beyond acknowledges the Traditional Owners of country throughout Australia, in particular the Wathaurong & Wurundjeri land in which we work. We pay our respect to their Elders past, present and emerging.

Beyond is an allied health clinic that provides quality healthcare in an environment that is safe and inclusive for people of all sexual orientations, sex and gender identities.

Our team is committed to a patient focused care model, we work as a team to get the best health outcomes for you.
